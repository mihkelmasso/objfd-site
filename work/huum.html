HUUM Heater

Electric sauna heater built around exposed stone mass

The HUUM heater explores a stripped-back approach to sauna design, where structure is minimized and the stone becomes the dominant visual and functional element.

The design is grounded in real manufacturing constraints and user experience — balancing aesthetics, durability, and heat performance.
The open structure allows airflow and heat distribution while showcasing the natural texture of the stones. The minimal frame reduces visual noise and emphasizes material honesty.
